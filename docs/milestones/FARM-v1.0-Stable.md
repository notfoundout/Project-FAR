## Boundary Rules

FARM coordinates requirements, defects, change review, and integration records.

FARM does not redefine FARA, FAR, FARO, or FARE.

FARM does not replace operations, methodology, architecture, or mathematics handled by the other framework layers.

---

## Change Policy

No new FARM primitives may be introduced without a demonstrated cross-framework requirement.

Stable FARM documents require formal review before modification.

Future development should be driven by concrete worked examples and downstream requirements, not speculative expansion.
