## Status

Achieved

## Purpose

This milestone establishes the first stable mathematical foundation of the Formal Architecture of Reasoning Evaluation.

The objective of this milestone is to provide a mathematically consistent base upon which the remainder of Project FAR may be constructed.

Future mathematical work shall proceed only when required by the canonical FAR framework.

---

## Stable Components

The following components are designated Stable.

### Repository Architecture

- Mathematics repository structure
- Directory organization
- Dependency organization

### Core Documentation

- `frameworks/FARE/mathematics/notation.md`
- `frameworks/FARE/mathematics/proof-policy.md`
- `frameworks/FARE/mathematics/theorem-index.md`

### Mathematical Definitions

- MDEF-001 through MDEF-010

These definitions constitute the initial mathematical vocabulary of FARE.

Changes shall occur only through formal review.

### Proof Infrastructure

- Proof library structure
- Theorem organization
- Proof governance
- Validation rules

### Accepted Foundation Theorems

The accepted subset of MT-001 through MT-020 is designated Stable.

Draft theorems remain research artifacts and are not part of the frozen mathematical foundation.

---

## Freeze Policy

Effective immediately:

- No new mathematical definitions shall be introduced unless required by the canonical FAR framework.
- New mathematical theorems shall be introduced only when they justify, extend, or formally support FAR.
- Existing mathematical definitions shall not be modified except through formal review prompted by a demonstrated inconsistency, a proven logical deficiency, or a mathematical proof requiring revision.
- Repository organization may evolve provided it does not alter accepted mathematical meaning.
