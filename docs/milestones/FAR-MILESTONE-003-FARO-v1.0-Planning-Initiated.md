# FAR-MILESTONE-003 — FARO v1.0 Planning Initiated

## Status

Initiated

---

## Date

2026-07-04

---

## Purpose

This milestone records the transition from FAR v1.0 Stable to FARO v1.0 planning.

FARO development begins only after FAR has been stabilized and formally recorded as the canonical methodology layer of Project FAR.

---

## Starting Conditions

FARO v1.0 planning begins after:

- FARE Mathematics v0.1 has been frozen;
- FAR Phase 1 canonical audit has been completed;
- FAR Phase 2 structural audit has been completed;
- FAR Phase 3 methodology audit has been completed;
- FAR Phase 4 consistency audit has been completed;
- FAR v1.0 Stable has been recorded.

---

## Development Target

FARO v1.0 should operationalize FAR v1.0 Stable.

It should define operations over FAR investigation artifacts and FARA representations without redefining FAR methodology or FARA architecture.

---

## Initial Objectives

FARO v1.0 planning should define:

- FARO scope;
- operational responsibilities;
- required inputs and outputs;
- audit procedures over FAR investigations;
- comparison procedures over FAR investigations;
- disagreement analysis procedures;
- reporting format;
- validation automation boundaries;
- dependency relation to FAR v1.0 Stable.

---

## Boundary Rules

FARO may operationalize FAR.

FARO may not redefine FAR.

FARO may operate over FARA representations.

FARO may not redefine FARA architecture.

FARO may expose future mathematical needs.

FARO may not expand FARE without a specific requirement and formal review.

---

## Notes

This milestone initiates planning only.

It does not declare FARO v1.0 complete or stable.
