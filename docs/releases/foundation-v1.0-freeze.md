# Foundation v1.0 Declaration

Foundation v1.0 is frozen as the accepted Phase 1 mathematical foundation of Project FAR.

Foundation v1.0 consists exactly of the accepted primitive(s), accepted canonical definitions, AX-001, L-001 through L-008, P-001 through P-008, and T-001 through T-015 explicitly enumerated in this release declaration. No other mathematical artifacts are part of Foundation v1.0 unless explicitly listed in this document.

This release declaration consumes the accepted Phase 1 evidence and does not reopen validation decisions, validate new mathematics, create new mathematics, or modify accepted theorem, proposition, lemma, axiom, primitive, definition, proof, dependency, or doctrine wording.

# Purpose

The purpose of this document is to freeze the validated Project FAR mathematical foundation as Foundation v1.0, provide the canonical release declaration, enumerate every mathematical artifact included in the release, identify the accepted validation basis, record repository-integrity verification, and preserve remaining non-blocking research questions for future work.

# Scope

Foundation v1.0 includes only the frozen artifacts listed below:

- Accepted primitive(s) recorded in the accepted primitive and AX-001 validation campaign.
- Accepted canonical definitions DEF-001 through DEF-089.
- AX-001.
- L-001 through L-008.
- P-001 through P-008.
- T-001 through T-015.

P-009 and any proposed, research, provisional, archival, or unlisted artifact is outside Foundation v1.0.

# Deferred Research Questions

The following research questions remain non-blocking and do not affect Foundation v1.0 validity:

- Future primitive-pressure investigations, including continued comparison of Operation with live primitive rivals and any later objectively demonstrated reducer.
- Future minimality refinements, including stricter removal tests for retained derivable or expository canonical interfaces.
- Future mechanized verification, including proof-assistant encodings and independent machine checking of accepted proof objects.

These questions are deferred research work. They do not block this release, do not alter the accepted Foundation v1.0 artifact set, and do not affect Foundation v1.0 validity.

# Change Control

Any future mathematical modification to a Foundation v1.0 primitive, definition, axiom, lemma, proposition, theorem, proof, dependency, or doctrine requires:

- a new validation campaign,
- updated validation reports,
- updated dependency review,
- updated proof review,
- explicit supersession of Foundation v1.0.

Routine documentation improvements remain allowed when they do not alter accepted mathematical content, accepted dependency content, accepted proof content, accepted doctrine wording, or the frozen artifact set.
