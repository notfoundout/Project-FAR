# Audit — USD-W5 minimality and equivalence

## Freeze integrity

The candidate universe, successful set, commitment-equivalence relation, translation requirements, cost preorder, admissible helpers, forbidden helpers, and tie rules were fixed before classification.

## Equivalence integrity

Equivalence requires total bidirectional translations with round-trip commitment preservation. Output agreement, common labels, and fixture-level behavioral similarity are explicitly insufficient.

## Cost integrity

No scalar score was introduced. Required primitive commitments, derived machinery, operations, and semantic description length remain separate. Hidden interpreters, decoders, bridges, canonicalizers, and reconstruction invariants are charged.

## Frontier integrity

GREL and ARG-HIST are removed only by registered dominance or preservation failure. FARA and LTS-PROV remain on the frontier because neither dominates the other. Their tradeoff is retained as incomparability rather than converted into an overall winner.

## Classification integrity

The correct terminal result is `multiple_incomparable_minima` inside the frozen candidate universe. `unique_minimum` is refuted only for that universe. Global minimality and exhaustive no-go remain unresolved.

## Claim boundary

This internal audit does not establish independent review, an exhaustive candidate universe, global minimality, uniqueness, universal structure, actual-process correspondence, or cross-context replication.
