# PBTS-001 REP-001 RUN-001 Coordinator-Control Audit

## Scope

This audit verifies that `PBTS-001-REP-001-RUN-001` can accept real independent replicators without fabricating identities, silently weakening independence, exposing submissions, or opening the scoped representation-theorem gate before evidence exists.

## Baseline preservation

The change does not modify:

- the reasoning-domain specification;
- IRD-001;
- PB-001;
- PBTS-001;
- PBTS-001 RUN-001 records or interpretation;
- the independent replication package or response schema;
- Foundation, FARA, or FARO mathematics;
- CRE-004 controls.

## Participant-integrity audit

Result: PASS for control freeze.

- REP-A, REP-B, and REP-C remain opaque empty slots.
- No name, affiliation, identity digest, calibration score, or independence classification is invented.
- Simulated personas, repeated passes by one agent, and project-authored isolated implementations are prohibited from counting as distinct R3 replicators.
- Private identity custody, when used, requires a public digest and sufficient auditor access to detect duplicate or fictitious enrollment.

## Exposure and blinding audit

Result: PASS with declared limitation.

- The repository is public, so technical blinding is not claimed.
- Prior access to RUN-001 judgments must be declared.
- Exposed submissions remain preserved but cannot automatically count as R3.
- Participant-facing bundles explicitly exclude RUN-001 result artifacts and derivative judgment summaries.

## Competence audit

Result: PASS for prospective control.

- The frozen six-item calibration remains unchanged.
- Qualification requires five of six correct and the critical observation-versus-inference item.
- Self-scoring is prohibited.
- One retest is allowed.
- Calibration is not treated as evidence of independence or scientific correctness.

## Assignment audit

Result: PASS for prospective control.

- Every primary slot receives the complete suite.
- Only section order is randomized.
- The deterministic algorithm and seed template are frozen before registration.
- Identity, affiliation, prior result, or expected conclusion cannot affect assignment.
- No assignments are generated before qualification.

## Append-only audit

Result: PASS.

Nine distinct channels exist for registrations, exposure declarations, calibration, independence audits, assignments, submissions, rejections or withdrawals, adjudications, and results.

Every channel:

- is marked append-only;
- contains an empty record list;
- uses the stable RUN-001 identifier;
- prohibits silent replacement through the governing registry.

The results channel remains aggregation-locked, and the submissions channel retains the mutual embargo.

## Role-separation audit

Result: PASS as a prospective rule, not an achieved fact.

The controls prohibit a primary replicator from:

- scoring their own calibration;
- auditing their own independence;
- adjudicating their own dispute;
- aggregating results before submission freeze.

No actual role-holder is registered.

## Gate audit

Result: PASS.

The only true execution-control field is that append-only channels are operational. The following remain false:

- three real registrants recorded;
- all calibrations passed;
- all exposure declarations recorded;
- independence audits complete;
- assignments generated;
- participant bundles verified;
- execution authorized.

All result gates remain false, including the representation-theorem gate.

## Machine-enforcement audit

`tools/check_pbts001_replication_run001_controls.py` checks:

- frozen package status;
- exact run and slot identities;
- empty participant and result fields;
- calibration and assignment rules;
- all channel paths and empty append-only state;
- mutual embargo and aggregation lock;
- false execution and result gates;
- explicit public-repository and nonindependence language;
- absence of affirmative PB-001, FARA, or theorem claims.

## Scientific status

The project has a runnable coordinator-control structure but no independent replicators and no replication evidence.

This is infrastructure enabling R3 recruitment and execution. It is not R3 evidence, PB-001 confirmation, P8 resolution, FARA validation, or a representation theorem.

## Required next action

Recruit at least three real qualified replicators, append their registrations and prior-exposure declarations, administer calibration, complete independent audits, and generate deterministic assignments. Replication judgments remain prohibited until those gates are satisfied.
