# FARO Phase 9 — Consistency Audit

## Status

Initiated.

---

## Purpose

This phase is the final FARO audit gate before FARO may be considered for v1.0 Stable status.

The objective is to verify that FARO's architecture, methodology, operation taxonomy, interface standards, and boundary rules are internally consistent and synchronized with FAR v1.0 Stable, FARA, and frozen FARE Mathematics v0.1.

This phase does not automatically declare FARO v1.0 Stable.

---

## Scope

Phase 9 reviews the FARO README, architecture, dependency graph, design principles, operation taxonomy, operation interface standard, operation category documents, v1.0 criteria, prior FARO audits, canonical map, project status, and framework boundaries.

---

## Audit Questions

Phase 9 checks for undefined operational terms, duplicate definitions, conflicting definitions, category ambiguity, missing operation interfaces, boundary violations, synchronization errors, circular dependencies, unsupported stability claims, and blockers to FARO v1.0 Stable.

---

## Initial State

Phase 9 begins after Phase 8 methodology audit.

FARO has passed architecture stabilization and methodology audit, but it remains pre-v1.0 until final consistency review is complete.

---

## Current Verdict

Phase 9 is initiated but not complete.

The next step is to perform final consistency review and record pass/fail findings against FARO v1.0 criteria.
