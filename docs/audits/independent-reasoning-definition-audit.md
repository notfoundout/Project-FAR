# Independent Reasoning Definition Audit

## Scope

This audit records the prospective review of IRD-001 before later preservation and representation work.

## Inputs

- frozen Reasoning Domain Specification v1.0;
- reasoning-domain registry D1-D16;
- Project FAR Research Architecture;
- anti-self-validation, evidence, observation, negative-control, cost, and anti-reintroduction standards.

## Dependency result

IRD-001 depends on the frozen reasoning domain and architecture-neutral governance. It does not depend on:

- FARA primitive definitions;
- FARO execution semantics;
- FAR-IR;
- candidate compilers or verifiers;
- prior favorable mappings;
- CRE-003 or CRE-004 outcomes.

This prevents a later representation theorem from defining its source class through the target architecture.

## Formal result recorded

IRD-001 introduces a process presentation with:

- partial temporal or event order;
- process states and histories;
- declared observations;
- commitment states;
- changing stakes;
- admissible evolutions;
- grounds and constraints;
- a testable support relation.

A reasoning episode requires R1-R6: situated stake, alternatives, ground sensitivity, directed evaluation or transformation, commitment consequence, and traceable justificatory dependence.

These are frozen hypotheses, not established necessary or sufficient conditions.

## Domain coverage audit

The definition does not require:

- discrete state;
- deterministic transition;
- explicit symbols;
- a single agent;
- centralized control;
- fixed representational types;
- fixed goals;
- fixed rules;
- termination;
- full observability;
- purely internal state;
- deductive support.

Accordingly, all frozen D1-D16 classes can be posed against IRD-001 without being excluded by substrate or candidate vocabulary. Actual adequacy remains untested.

## Triviality controls

Ten mandatory countermodels are frozen. They target:

- arbitrary relabeling;
- finite lookup equivalence;
- post hoc explanations;
- hidden human operators;
- ungrounded optimization;
- trivial universal encoding;
- distributed composition;
- self-modification;
- continuous embodied control;
- defeasible normative conflict.

These controls ensure that broad expressibility alone cannot count as success.

## Observability audit

E0-E2 grades separate behavioral compatibility from operational grounding and proved or validated correspondence. The definition prohibits invented hidden transitions and limits claims to the observation contract.

Opaque systems may remain unresolved rather than being excluded or repaired.

## Candidate-neutrality audit

No R1-R6 clause requires a FARA primitive. Each clause is justified through frozen domain pressures and can be modeled using multiple mathematical formalisms.

The definition permits all of the following outcomes:

- successful scoped support;
- revision or rejection of IRD-001;
- multiple non-equivalent reasoning classes;
- no finite universal architecture;
- insufficient evidence.

## Scientific nonclaims

This audit does not establish:

- adequacy of IRD-001;
- completeness or independence of R1-R6;
- the current preservation basis;
- a representation theorem;
- FARA universality, necessity, minimality, economy, or superiority;
- independent replication.

## Next audit target

The preservation-basis investigation must derive preservation commitments from IRD-001, test the current six dimensions through distinction pairs and ablations, and preserve the possibility that the correct basis has fewer, more, or differently organized dimensions.
