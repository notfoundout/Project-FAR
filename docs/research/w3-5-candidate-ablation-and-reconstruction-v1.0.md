## Method

Each atomic record contains candidate presence, evidence location, commitment-level ablation, recomputed discriminator output, six-dimensional preservation, exact reconstruction, commitment-equivalence comparison, equivalent-reintroduction status, machinery cost, ambiguities, and evidence references.

The result separately derives primitive necessity, explicit-representation necessity, structural-commitment necessity, reasoning specificity, and architecture invariance. Genericity never entails structural dispensability. Derivability may refute primitive necessity while the reconstructed commitment still supports structural necessity.
