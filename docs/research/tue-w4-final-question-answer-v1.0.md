# TUE-W4 Final Internal Answer

## Question

Is there a nontrivial universal structure underlying reasoning?

## Final internal answer

Yes, for the maximal internally adjudicable natural class defined by Project FAR.

For normatively assessable reasoning systems whose relevant reasoning facts can be specified, tested, and uniformly recovered through effective representations satisfying the full reasoning-faithfulness contract, **RCCD is the necessary, nontrivial, and registered-level irreducible universal kernel**.

RCCD requires:

1. recoverable commitments;
2. constrained admissible evolution;
3. dependency-sensitive revision;
4. historical identity;
5. uniform effective recovery.

## Why this is the terminal answer

The prior internal program established the RCCD necessity result for the natural class. The terminal extension then tested the remaining ways that result could change.

TUE-W1 resolved instrumented creative hypothesis generation within RCCD, while preserving tacit moral salience and inaccessible processes as principled evidential Unknowns. Unknown is neither Pass nor Fail.

TUE-W2 attacked all five registered defeating conditions. None was established. The campaign did not find a sixth irreducible primitive, a faithful effective RCCD escape, circular construction of C* or P*, a non-reconstructible plural kernel, or a removable RCCD component.

TUE-W3 tested ten proposed deeper substrates. None produced a strict non-vacuous reduction. The strongest explanatory compression restated the five RCCD obligations and therefore did not derive them from a smaller independent basis.

## Exact scope

The positive result is relative to:

- the natural class C* of normatively assessable reasoning systems;
- the full reasoning-faithfulness contract P*;
- the broadened uniform effective representation family E*;
- the rule that all decoders, schedulers, histories, codebooks, proof objects, closure procedures, interpretation maps, and query interfaces are charged as machinery.

“Maximal” means maximal within the registered internally assessable effective scope. It does not mean every conceivable inaccessible, non-effective, oracle-defined, or future process.

## Evidential boundary

Where the relevant reasoning facts cannot be recovered or discriminated, the universal-structure question cannot presently be adjudicated. Such cases do not support RCCD and do not refute it.

Therefore the unrestricted question over all conceivable processes is evidentially underdetermined, while the maximal effective and assessable question has a positive answer.

## Defeating conditions

The answer must be reopened if concrete evidence establishes any of the following:

1. an assessable in-scope system requiring a genuine sixth irreducible primitive;
2. a faithful uniform effective representation satisfying P* without RCCD-equivalent commitments;
3. circular definition of C* or P* by R1-R5;
4. two successful kernels not bidirectionally reconstructible without hidden primitive machinery;
5. removal of one of R1-R5 under P* without preservation loss.

## Program closure

This PR closes `POST-SC-TUE-001`. No additional internal PR is authorized merely to add supportive mappings, representations, or candidate families. Further work requires either a defeating-condition witness, a separately registered empirical actual-process program, or external review and replication.

## Nonclaims

This result is not a claim of metaphysical fundamentality, actual cognitive or physical implementation, external validation, or unrestricted universality over inaccessible and non-effective processes.
