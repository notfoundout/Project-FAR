# SC-W2 Reasoning Boundary-Case Adjudication

## Purpose

Test whether the W9 class excludes plausible reasoning systems merely because they do not present themselves in RCCD vocabulary.

## Neutral membership criterion

A system is presumptively within the reasoning target when it performs normatively assessable selection, inference, revision, planning, comparison, or justification over distinguishable alternatives. This criterion does not require propositions, explicit rules, provenance graphs, histories, or RCCD terminology.

A system is not declared non-reasoning merely because its internals are inaccessible. Opaque cases are marked unassessable. Semantic commitments may be reconstructed only when source evidence independently warrants them.

## Cases

The frozen set covers reactive policies, model-free reinforcement learning, analogical transfer, embodied continuous control, opaque input-output systems, distributed swarms, legal defeasibility, Bayesian revision, creative hypothesis generation, associative transformation, self-modifying intentions, and multi-agent negotiation.

No assessable case required a sixth shared kernel primitive. Continuous state, probability, similarity geometry, authority hierarchies, agent indexing, common ground, and distributed realization were chargeable as domain parameters or derived machinery.

No assessable faithful case eliminated one of R1-R5. Apparent eliminations either lost a registered reasoning distinction or moved recovery into hidden decoders, update traces, trajectory identity, or collective-state machinery.

## Hard boundaries

Two classes remain blocking rather than supportive:

1. Opaque input-output systems whose relevant internal distinctions are unavailable even in principle from the evidence supplied.
2. Creative generation cases where candidate outputs are visible but the grounds of generation are absent or inaccessible.

These cases do not count as RCCD successes. They limit evidential reach and remain candidates for later downgrade.

## Result

`no_boundary_case_yet_requires_new_kernel_primitive_but_evidential_boundary_must_remain_open`

The naturalized class can be stated independently of RCCD. The current boundary search does not refute R1-R5, but it does not establish the final answer. The next decisive test varies the preservation contract itself rather than adding further examples.
