# SC-W3 Preservation-Contract Ladder

## Question

Does RCCD remain necessary because it captures unavoidable reasoning distinctions, or only because the preservation contract already demands RCCD-shaped information?

## Method

The analysis varies the preservation contract while holding the represented systems and machinery-accounting rules fixed. A component is counted as necessary only when omitting it collapses two systems that differ on a query authorized by the active contract.

Contract duties are stated without RCCD primitive names. Decoders, schedulers, lookup tables, provenance closure, canonicalizers, query-specific procedures, and hidden historical reconstruction are charged rather than treated as free interpretation.

## Ladder result

The weakest extensional contract preserves only registered input-output outcomes. It forces no RCCD component; only distinguishable observations and an effective input-output association remain.

Adding present commitment recovery and a uniform finite-query method forces R1 and R5. Adding permitted-versus-forbidden change forces R2. Adding revision consequences under defeated grounds forces R3. Adding supersession identity and path-dependent future admissibility forces R4.

The full reasoning-faithfulness contract therefore forces R1-R5, but each component disappears from the necessity claim when its corresponding preservation duty is removed.

## Alternative contracts

A behavioral-prediction contract permits semantic, dependency, and historical collapse. A provenance-only contract preserves R2-R5 without forcing interpreted commitment identity. A semantic trajectory contract without grounds preserves R1, R2, R4, and R5 but not R3. A nonuniform local-query contract preserves R1-R4 without forcing R5.

## Interpretation

RCCD is not contract-independent. That is not itself a defect: necessity claims are always relative to what faithful representation must preserve.

The relevant anti-circularity result is narrower. The full contract's duties are independently intelligible reasoning facts, and each duty has a paired-system separation witness. The contract does not merely name RCCD components or require their preferred encoding.

Accordingly, this workstream classifies RCCD as contract-relative but not shown construct-loaded under the independently stated full reasoning-faithfulness contract.

## Generic residue

Across every tested contract, only a weak residue survives: distinguishable items or observations connected by some effective association. That structure is too weak to preserve reasons, revision, admissibility, or historical identity and therefore cannot answer the richer universal-structure question by itself.

## Result

`rccd_is_contract_relative_but_not_shown_construct_loaded_under_independently_justified_full_contract`

## Claim boundary

The W9 theorem must name the full preservation profile or an extensionally equivalent one. This result does not establish RCCD under outcome-only or prediction-only faithfulness, does not validate the contract externally, and does not issue the final internal answer.
