# IKD-W4 Cross-Feature Composition v1.0

## Question

Does RCCD-001 remain jointly realizable when the six positive USD-W1 feature families occur simultaneously, rather than only in separately tested subclasses?

The six families are infinite carriers, continuous dynamics, partial observability, open-ended histories, semantic change, and non-finite-support stochasticity.

## Why direct composition was required

Separate featurewise representation results do not imply closure under conjunction. Interfaces may conflict: continuous and discrete clocks can disagree; semantic versions may not align with observation histories; stochastic kernels may be nonmeasurable relative to the observation interface; and infinite-support approximations may destroy historical or semantic distinctions.

IKD-W4 therefore executes joint constructions instead of multiplying six prior positive results.

## Frozen compatibility contract

A composed system must provide:

1. shared identity for reachable configurations and events;
2. semantic versions defined on every reachable commitment-bearing state;
3. computable and measurable observation and probability interfaces;
4. finite, prefix-coherent history queries;
5. declared hybrid flow and jump semantics;
6. explicit updates to grounds and dependencies;
7. complete charging of adapters, schedulers, tail certificates, filters, and canonicalizers;
8. no future-information, exact-infinite-sum, or source-specific decoding oracle.

These are substantive scope conditions. They are not assumed to hold for arbitrary mathematical conjunctions.

## Executed matrix

The workstream executed:

- all 15 pairwise conjunctions;
- all 20 triple conjunctions;
- all six five-feature leave-one-out conjunctions;
- one all-six conjunction.

Every registered case passed on the frozen effective compatibility class.

## All-six construction

The all-six witness uses a countably presented stochastic hybrid configuration with:

- semantic-versioned commitments;
- explicit dependency and ground records;
- partial observation state;
- computable probability-kernel state with certified tails;
- append-only revision history;
- continuous flows and discrete jumps governed by declared guards and invariants.

A single finite-query recovery interface exposes current commitments, admissibility, dependencies, semantic versions, bounded probability queries, and finite historical prefixes to requested certified precision. The interface does not reveal hidden or future state.

This preserves each RCCD commitment:

- recoverable commitment content;
- constrained admissible evolution;
- dependency-preserving explicit revision;
- queryable historical identity;
- uniform bounded-query recovery.

## Interaction results

Continuous dynamics and semantic change compose when semantic switches occur at declared hybrid events or through an effectively specified continuous semantic interface.

Partial observability and semantic change compose when observation provenance records the semantic version and observational equivalence is not treated as semantic identity.

Stochasticity and open-ended history compose for finite-prefix and bounded computable queries. Unrestricted exact infinite-horizon queries remain outside scope.

Infinite carriers and non-finite-support stochasticity compose when carriers are effectively enumerable and probability tails are certified.

Continuous, partially observed stochastic dynamics compose when filtering and positive-evidence conditioning are computable.

The all-six case composes under the full compatibility contract. This does not imply closure for arbitrary noncomputable, nonmeasurable, oracle-defined, or interface-incompatible systems.

## Negative controls

The execution rejects:

- independent feature encodings without shared identity;
- incompatible time bases;
- nonmeasurable observation kernels;
- retroactive semantic-history rewriting;
- finite truncation presented as exact infinite-support probability;
- future-history access;
- hidden schedulers or adapters;
- inference of joint success solely from separate featurewise passes.

## Result

The terminal classification is:

`bounded_cross_feature_compositional_closure_supported_with_explicit_compatibility_conditions`

RCCD-001 is jointly realizable across all six registered feature families on the frozen effective hybrid subclass. This advances the discovery program materially, but it does not establish arbitrary conjunction closure, uniqueness, global necessity, global minimality, actual-process correspondence, or universal structure.
