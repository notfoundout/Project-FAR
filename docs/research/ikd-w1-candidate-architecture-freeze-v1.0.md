# IKD-W1 Candidate Architecture Freeze v1.0

## Status

Frozen prospectively and unexecuted by PR #261.

## Objective

Expand the universal-kernel search beyond FARA, LTS-PROV, GREL, and ARG-HIST without selecting candidates because they resemble or favor the current successful frontier.

The freeze admits six independently motivated architecture families before scoring:

1. `PROC-CAT-001` — compositional process and categorical semantics;
2. `CAUSAL-SCM-001` — structural causal and intervention semantics;
3. `PROOF-TYPE-001` — typed judgments and proof objects;
4. `INFO-FLOW-001` — information access, channels, and epistemic updates;
5. `COALG-DYN-001` — coalgebraic behavior and coinductive state evolution;
6. `ALG-REWRITE-001` — algebraic laws, equations, and rewriting.

## Material novelty

Different terminology or implementation structure is insufficient. A candidate must declare an independently motivated ontology, operations, semantic commitments, admissibility conditions, and failure modes. It is rejected when its operative commitments are merely a renaming, compilation, wrapper, or hidden reconstruction of FARA or LTS-PROV.

A candidate must expose every helper, decoder, adapter, invariant, semantic bridge, history structure, and reconstruction mechanism. Equivalent reintroduction is charged rather than treated as elimination.

## Frozen comparison contract

All candidates face the same bounded source scope, six-dimensional preservation contract, negative controls, and machinery ledger. Results remain dimensioned. No scalar overall score or preferred-vocabulary tie breaker is permitted.

The cost coordinates are supplied primitives, derived machinery, operations, semantic description length, and equivalent reintroduction. Tradeoffs remain incomparable unless one candidate satisfies registered Pareto dominance.

## Admission boundaries

Admission occurs before candidate scoring. No candidate may receive:

- a source-scope exception;
- a repaired primitive set after seeing results;
- an unrestricted interpreter;
- a source-specific decoder;
- arbitrary metadata as an escape hatch;
- free imported machinery from FARA or LTS-PROV.

At least one distinctive constraint or failure mode must follow from the candidate's own conceptual basis.

## Execution gate

Merging this freeze authorizes `IKD-W2-EXPANDED-COMPETITION`. It does not authorize a common-kernel claim, frontier update, or universal-structure conclusion before the expanded competition is executed.

## Nonclaims

This freeze does not establish that the candidate universe is exhaustive, that any new candidate succeeds, that FARA or LTS-PROV is displaced, that a deeper common factor exists, or that global universality or minimality has been established.
