# SC-W4 Broader Effective Representation Escape Search v1.0

## Question

Can a representation satisfy the independently justified full reasoning-faithfulness contract while avoiding commitments equivalent to RCCD R1-R5?

A genuine escape must do all three things:

1. preserve current reasoning content, constrained change, revision-relevant grounds, supersession history, and uniform finite recovery;
2. provide an effective family-wide construction and recovery procedure;
3. omit the corresponding commitments without moving them into hidden decoders, schedulers, proof annotations, codebooks, canonicalizers, or query machinery.

Literal field names and RCCD syntax are irrelevant. Commitment equivalence is decisive.

## Search expansion

The search covers relational, algebraic, coalgebraic, event-sourced, proof-theoretic, probabilistic, continuous, distributed, latent-state, executable-trace, category-theoretic, and database/information-flow families.

This is substantially broader than the named W8 architecture comparison. Each family is evaluated at the level of preservation duties rather than surface ontology.

## Escape adjudication

### Relational and algebraic families

These families can rename or redistribute the kernel, but faithful interpretations still require content-bearing entities or valuations, admissibility structure, dependency structure, historical ordering, and effective query interpretation. They are alternative realizations, not escapes.

### Coalgebraic and behavioral families

Pure behavioral equivalence is too weak for the full contract because it collapses equal-output systems with different grounds or histories. Observation algebras enriched enough to answer the required questions reconstruct the omitted distinctions.

### Event logs and executable traces

A raw event stream is insufficient. Current projections, guards, causal links, event ordering, and replay or instrumentation procedures are required. Those commitments implement R1-R5 in event-sourced form.

### Proof and type-theoretic families

Judgments, rules, proof dependencies, derivation identity, and normalization or elimination procedures provide direct commitment-equivalent realizations of the kernel.

### Probabilistic, continuous, and hybrid families

Probability and continuity alter the domain machinery, not the preservation obligations. Faithful recovery requires measurable content regions or valuations, support or viability constraints, dependency or sensitivity structure, trajectory history, and effective observations.

### Distributed and multi-agent families

The kernel may be distributed. Local and collective commitments, protocols, authority or dependence links, interaction histories, and aggregation queries remain necessary under the full contract.

### Compressed latent and learned representations

A certified uniform decoder that recovers the registered distinctions is charged as representation machinery and reconstructs RCCD-equivalent commitments. A source-specific, adaptive, opaque, or uncertified decoder does not establish faithful uniform recovery. Such cases remain unresolved rather than being counted as escapes.

## Adversarial escape mechanisms

The search explicitly tests output quotients, final-snapshot compression, transition-only and provenance-only structures, per-instance lookup tables, oracle decoders, uncomputable codes, hidden schedulers, implicit causal annotations, uncharged version tags, and adaptive learned decoders.

Every registered attempt falls into one of three categories:

- preservation failure;
- equivalent reintroduction through charged machinery;
- exclusion from the effective family because recovery is nonuniform, oracle-dependent, or non-effective.

## Result

`no_faithful_effective_representation_escape_found_across_broadened_families_without_rccd_equivalent_reintroduction`

The bounded necessity result survives this broader search. This does not enumerate every mathematically possible formalism. It establishes that changing representational language, ontology, compression method, or implementation style does not evade the kernel when the full independently justified preservation contract and effective uniform recovery are retained.

## Remaining boundary

The principal unresolved region is opaque learned or physical systems for which no certified family-wide recovery method exists. They cannot presently be classified as RCCD successes or genuine escapes.

The next workstream is SC-W5: frozen held-out cross-context testing for primitive extensions and preservation failures.
