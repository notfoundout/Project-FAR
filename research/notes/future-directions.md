# Future Directions

## Purpose

This document records potential long-term directions for Project FAR.

Unlike the active research questions, these directions are not required for the completion of the core framework. They represent opportunities for future expansion once the architecture, methodology, and formal theory have matured.

---

# Formal Theory

## Automated Proof Verification

Investigate whether the formal theory of Project FAR can be represented within proof assistants such as Lean, Coq, or Isabelle.

---

## Formal Dependency Analysis

Automatically generate dependency graphs for definitions, axioms, propositions, lemmas, theorems, and proofs.

---

## Meta-Theory

Develop a formal theory describing the properties of Project FAR itself.

Possible topics include:

- Consistency
- Completeness
- Independence
- Minimality
- Expressive equivalence

---
