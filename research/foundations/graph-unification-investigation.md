# Graph Unification Investigation

## Purpose

Determine whether the graph structures identified throughout Project FAR represent distinct mathematical objects or specializations of a single abstract graph structure.

The objective is to identify whether a universal graph abstraction underlies the theory.

---

---

These are specializations of one universal graph abstraction.

- node types;
- edge types;

# Evaluation

Every investigated graph appears to consist of:

- typed nodes;
- typed edges;
- constraints governing admissible structure.

The mathematical structure itself appears unchanged.
