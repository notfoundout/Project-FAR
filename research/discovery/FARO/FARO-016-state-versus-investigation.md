# FARO Discovery Investigation

## Identifier

FARO-016

---

# Title

State versus Investigation

---

## Status

In Progress

---

# Purpose

Determine whether operational reasoning fundamentally transforms reasoning states or investigations.

The objective is to identify the true operational substrate of reasoning.

---

# Dependencies

- FARO-001 through FARO-015

---

# Central Question

During reasoning, what actually changes?

---

# Candidate 1

Reasoning State

Definition:

A snapshot of an investigation at a particular moment.

---

# Candidate 2

Investigation

Definition:

The complete reasoning objective together with its evolving structure.

---

# Test Case

Suppose an investigation begins.

State S₀

↓

Apply an inference.

↓

State S₁

↓

Apply another inference.

↓

State S₂

---

# Question 1

Did the investigation change?

Observation:

The investigation remained the same investigation.

Its objective did not change.

Result:

No.

---

# Question 2

Did the reasoning state change?

Observation:

Representations changed.

Relations changed.

Admissibility changed.

Available operations changed.

Result:

Yes.

---

# Question 3

Can multiple reasoning states belong to one investigation?

Result:

Yes.

---

# Question 4

Can one reasoning state belong to multiple investigations?

Current evidence:

Undetermined.

Requires further investigation.

---

# Analysis

The investigation provides continuity.

Reasoning states provide progression.

Progression therefore appears to occur through states rather than investigations.

---

# Competing Hypotheses

## H1

Reasoning transforms investigations.

---

## H2

Reasoning transforms reasoning states.

---

# Evaluation

An investigation may persist while numerous reasoning states occur.

A reasoning state cannot persist unchanged if reasoning progresses.

Accordingly, progression appears to occur through reasoning states.

---

# Decision Criteria

If investigations remain identifiable while reasoning progresses through multiple configurations, then reasoning states are the operational substrate.

---

# Provisional Conclusion

Current evidence favors reasoning states as the object of operational transformation.

Investigations provide identity.

Reasoning states provide progression.

---

# Remaining Questions

Determine:

- the formal definition of a reasoning state;
- state identity;
- state equivalence;
- state transition invariants.

---

# Current Status

The investigation remains active.

Current evidence favors reasoning states over investigations as the operational substrate.
