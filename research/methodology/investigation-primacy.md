# Investigation Primacy

## Purpose

Determine whether investigation is the fundamental methodological unit of Project FAR.

The objective is to establish whether every reasoning activity can be understood as occurring within an investigation.

---

# Motivation

Project FAR currently expresses:

- proof;
- validation;
- falsification;
- framework discovery;
- primitive identification;
- theorem development;

as investigations.

This document examines whether that common structure is accidental or fundamental.

---

# Central Question

Is every act of reasoning necessarily part of an investigation?

---

# Candidate Activity 1

Formal Proof

Question:

Can a proof exist without an investigation?

Evaluation:

A proof always seeks to establish a specific proposition.

The proposition defines an objective.

Result:

Appears to constitute an investigation.

---

# Candidate Activity 2

Framework Design

Question:

Can a framework be developed without investigating its required structure?

Evaluation:

Framework development consists of evaluating alternative architectural possibilities.

Result:

Appears to constitute an investigation.

---

# Candidate Activity 3

Validation

Question:

Can validation occur without determining whether some claim is true?

Evaluation:

Validation possesses an explicit objective and evaluation procedure.

Result:

Appears to constitute an investigation.

---

# Candidate Activity 4

Falsification

Question:

Can falsification occur without investigating whether a claim survives attempted refutation?

Evaluation:

No.

Result:

Appears to constitute an investigation.

---

# Candidate Activity 5

Primitive Identification

Question:

Evaluation:

No.

Result:

Appears to constitute an investigation.

---

# Pattern Analysis

Every examined reasoning activity possesses:

- an objective;
- a subject;
- an evaluation process;
- a possible conclusion.

These characteristics appear to define investigations.

No investigated reasoning activity exists independently of them.

---

# Competing Hypotheses

## H1

Investigation is one methodological tool among many.

---

## H2

Investigation is the universal methodological unit from which all reasoning activities are instantiated.

---

# Evaluation

Current evidence favors H2.

Proofs, validations, falsifications, framework discovery, theorem development, and primitive identification all appear to be specialized investigations.

---

# Consequences

If this conclusion withstands further investigation:

- investigation becomes the universal methodological abstraction of Project FAR;
- proofs become proof investigations;
- validations become validation investigations;
- falsifications become falsification investigations;
- framework design becomes framework investigations;
- theorem discovery becomes theorem investigations.

This unifies the methodology under a single conceptual framework.

---

# Remaining Questions

Future investigations should determine:

- the formal definition of an investigation;
- the minimal structure of an investigation;
- the lifecycle of investigations;
- the composition of investigations;
- whether investigations admit formal algebraic properties.

---

# Current Status

The investigation remains active.

Current evidence supports treating investigation as the fundamental methodological unit of Project FAR.
