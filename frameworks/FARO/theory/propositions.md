# FARO Propositions

## Purpose

This document records propositions derivable from the axioms and inference rules of FARO.

Propositions establish intermediate results used throughout the operational theory.

Unless elevated to theorem status, propositions should be regarded as supporting results rather than principal conclusions.

---

# Current Status

No canonical propositions have yet been adopted.

The operational theory has not yet been sufficiently axiomatized to permit formal derivation.

Future propositions shall include:

- operational properties;
- algebraic properties;
- semantic properties;
- rewrite properties; and
- normalization properties.
