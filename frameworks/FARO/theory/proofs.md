# FARO Proofs

## Purpose

This document records the formal proofs of propositions and theorems appearing elsewhere in the FARO theory.

Proofs should be complete, explicit, and independently verifiable.

Every proof shall reference:

- the canonical definitions;
- the canonical axioms;
- the applicable inference rules; and
- previously established propositions or theorems.

---

# Proof Format

Every proof shall contain:

1. Statement.
2. Assumptions.
3. Definitions Used.
4. Axioms Used.
5. Inference Rules Used.
6. Proof.
7. Conclusion.
