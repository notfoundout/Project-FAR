# FARO Operational Evaluation

## Purpose

This document defines operational evaluation within FARO.

Operational evaluation assesses operational qualities of FAR investigation artifacts using explicit FAR-grounded criteria.

---

## Definition

Operational evaluation is the assessment of investigation artifacts for qualities such as completeness, traceability, reconstructibility, explicitness, and procedural integrity.

---

## Non-Truth Rule

Operational evaluation does not determine whether a conclusion is true.

It determines whether the investigation artifacts satisfy specified operational criteria.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Operational evaluation may consider:

- workflow completion;
- artifact completeness;
- transition traceability;
- revision-record adequacy;
- closure-status adequacy;
- reconstructibility;
- explicitness;
- boundary compliance.

---

## Relationship to Auditing

Auditing checks compliance with validation requirements.

Operational evaluation may assess broader operational quality, provided the criteria are explicit and FAR-grounded.

---

## Relationship to FARE

Operational evaluation is not FARE mathematics.

If operational evaluation requires formal mathematical support, that requirement must be recorded before FARE expansion occurs.

---

## Boundary

Operational evaluation shall not redefine FAR methodology, FARA architecture, or FARE mathematics.
