# Mathematical Theorem

## Identifier

MT-001

---

# Title

Reachability Is Reflexive

---

# Status

Accepted

---

# Dependencies

- MDEF-002
- MDEF-003

---

# Statement

Every evaluation is reachable from itself.

---

# Proof

Every evaluation has an empty path from itself to itself by MDEF-003. Reachability is defined by existence of an evaluation path. Therefore every evaluation is reachable from itself.

---

# Notes

This does not establish symmetry.
