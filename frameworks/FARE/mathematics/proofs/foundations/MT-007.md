# Mathematical Theorem

## Identifier

MT-007

---

# Title

Every Neighborhood Contains Its Center

---

# Status

Accepted

---

# Objective

Show that each neighborhood contains the evaluation it is centered on.

---

# Dependencies

- MDEF-006

---

# Statement

For every evaluation `E`, every neighborhood `U` in `N(E)` contains `E`.

---

# Proof

MDEF-006 requires every neighborhood of `E` to contain `E`.

Therefore every neighborhood `U` in `N(E)` contains `E`.

---

# Corollaries

None.

---

# Consequences

Neighborhood systems have a self-containment condition.

---

# Notes

This does not imply that neighborhood systems form topologies.
