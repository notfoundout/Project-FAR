# FARE Terminology Audit

## Status

In Progress

---

# Purpose

Audit the terminology of the Formal Architecture of Reasoning Evaluation.

The objective is to ensure that every technical term possesses one canonical definition and is used consistently throughout the framework.

---

# Audit Scope

This audit evaluates:

- architectural terminology;
- assessment terminology;
- relationship terminology;
- lifecycle terminology;
- graph terminology;
- proof terminology.

---

# Audit Criteria

Every technical term shall satisfy the following requirements.

- Exactly one canonical definition.
- No conflicting meanings.
- No undefined usage.
- No unnecessary synonyms.
- Consistent usage throughout FARE.
