# Examples

## Purpose

This document provides illustrative examples of investigations conducted using the FAR methodology.

The examples demonstrate the structure of a FAR investigation rather than the correctness of any particular conclusion.

---

## Example Workflow

A typical FAR investigation proceeds through the following stages.

1. Define the investigation.
2. Identify the relevant representations.
3. Specify the interpretation.
4. Select the reasoning calculus.
5. Construct the initial reasoning state.
6. Perform explicit reasoning transitions.
7. Determine the admissibility structure.
8. Record the resolution.

---

## Example Investigations

Representative applications include:

- Scientific investigations
- Historical investigations
- Legal reasoning
- Political analysis
- Mathematical reasoning

Detailed case studies are maintained in the `examples/` directory.

---

## Purpose of Examples

Examples illustrate the methodology.

They do not constitute evidence for the correctness or completeness of the framework itself.
