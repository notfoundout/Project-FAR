# Dependency Principles

## Purpose

This document establishes the dependency rules governing the organization of Project FAR.

Its objective is to prevent circular development.

---

# Principle 1

Definitions shall not depend upon propositions.

---

# Principle 2

Axioms shall depend only upon definitions.

---

# Principle 3

Inference rules shall depend only upon definitions and axioms.

---

# Principle 4

Propositions shall depend only upon:

- definitions;
- axioms; and
- inference rules.

---

# Principle 5

Theorems may depend upon:

- definitions;
- axioms;
- inference rules;
- propositions; and
- previously established theorems.

---

# Principle 6

Proofs shall cite every dependency explicitly.

---

# Principle 7

Research documents shall never be cited as canonical authority.

Only canonical documents may justify canonical conclusions.

---

# Principle 8

Dependencies shall always point toward more fundamental documents.

Circular dependencies are prohibited.
