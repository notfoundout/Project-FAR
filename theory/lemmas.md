# Lemmas

## Purpose

This document records lemmas derived from the definitions, axioms, and propositions of Project FAR.

A lemma is an intermediate formal result established primarily to support the proof of one or more theorems.

Proofs are maintained separately.

---

## Current Status

No lemmas have yet been formally established.

As the theoretical development of Project FAR progresses, lemmas will be introduced only when they simplify or enable the proof of subsequent theorems.
