## Chronology

This extension was designed after the official CRE-002 result. It is prospectively governed by Vocabulary Semantics Baseline 1.1 and cannot serve as independent evidence for, retrospective validation of, or reclassification of CRE-002.

## Research question

Under Vocabulary Semantics Baseline 1.1, can each official candidate vocabulary construct, lower, replay, and behaviorally verify the frozen bounded scenario without undeclared semantic commitments or unrestricted hidden metalanguage?

## Hypotheses

- H-SCOPE: At least one candidate reaches `complete` under every frozen gate.
- H-REPRO: A reproducible candidate requires three independent deterministic regenerations with identical canonical outputs and source digests.
- H-BOUNDARY: Any unlicensed commitment must produce `partial` or `unsupported`, never silent semantic invention.

No necessity, minimality, independence, superiority, or universal-structure hypothesis is tested.

## Independence rule

Vocabulary-specific native constructs, mappings, lowering rules, and compiler code may be authored only after checksum lock and reviewed execution unlock. No frozen scientific input may be changed in response to implementation failure. Any such change creates a new experiment identifier.

## Required candidate artifacts

Each vocabulary must emit source and semantic checksums, a vocabulary-native representation, declared derived machinery, executable lowering rules, an atomic trace, trace-replay report, generated execution model, full bounded-state verifier report, compiler-boundary report, mutation-test report, deterministic-regeneration report, and limitation report.

## Outcome rule

Candidate outcomes are `complete`, `partial`, `unsupported`, or `error`. Behavioral agreement alone is insufficient: semantic licensing, trace replay, output preservation, deterministic regeneration, and mutation sensitivity must all pass.

## Stopping rule

Processing stops for a candidate when a required commitment cannot be licensed under Baseline 1.1 without undeclared semantics. The package may not be weakened to force completion.

## Analysis boundary

Results are reported per vocabulary. No ranking is permitted. The original CRE-002 outcome remains separate and unchanged.

## Nonclaims

No result from this extension alone establishes universal sufficiency, primitive-only sufficiency, necessity, minimality, independence, superiority, a FAR proof, universal reasoning structure, or that CRE-002 was erroneous.
