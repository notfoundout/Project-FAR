## Use

This worksheet records adjudication after mappings are collected. It must not be filled before evaluator submissions exist. Apply only the frozen protocol rules; do not invent new scoring, equivalence, preservation, Pareto, aggregation, or interpretation rules.

## Required Records

For each disputed item preserve both original judgments, disputed component, governing rule, final resolution, consensus status, and whether a third blinded adjudicator was involved.

## Completion Gate

Adjudication is complete only when disagreement, equivalence, preservation, Pareto, and aggregation worksheets are complete for all submitted mappings and ablation submissions.
