# CRE-001 Evaluator Instructions v1.0

Status: Frozen evaluator instruction package
Instruction identifier: CRE-001-INSTRUCTIONS-1.0
Experiment: CRE-001
Protocol: CRP-1.0

## Isolation Requirements

You will receive exactly one vocabulary package and the frozen scenario package. Do not seek, request, infer, or use information about other vocabularies, target identity, expected strength, previous mapping results, protocol rationale, or other evaluators' work.

## Prohibited Information

Do not use external commentary about Project FAR, prior external-validation reports, prior mappings, development discussions, comparative hypotheses, or expected outcomes. If prohibited information is encountered, stop and report a procedural violation.

## Submission Procedure

1. Confirm the protocol, scenario, vocabulary, instruction, canonicalization, and adjudication version identifiers supplied in the provenance template.
2. Complete the mapping submission template.
3. Complete the CIR submission template if assigned to provide CIR-ready output.
4. Declare every assumption, derived construct, failure, and `Unknown` value.
5. Submit only the completed official templates.

## Permitted Assumptions

You may assume the scenario text is complete, discrete, and fixed. You may assume that identifiers in the scenario are stable. You may not assume any transition, rule, proposition, dependency, output, or domain rule not stated in the scenario.

## Derived-Machinery Rules

Derived machinery is permitted only when declared with formal role, supplied category, preservation requirement served, necessity, and provenance. Derived machinery may not alter the scenario or introduce unstated domain rules.

## Output Requirements

Outputs must use the official mapping and CIR templates. All preservation arguments must explicitly identify `Pass`, `Partial`, `Fail`, or `Unknown` for structural, semantic, operational, dependency, information, and historical preservation. Every declared failure and every `Unknown` must include justification.

## Completion Checklist

- one vocabulary used;
- no other vocabulary consulted;
- scenario unchanged;
- assumptions declared;
- derived constructs declared;
- preservation arguments completed;
- provenance completed;
- failures declared;
- `Unknown` values declared;
- output templates completed.
