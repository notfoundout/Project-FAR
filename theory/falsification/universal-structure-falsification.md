# Universal Structure Falsification

Status: Provisional

FAR's primitive architecture fails when an explicit reasoning system cannot be represented by the accepted primitive structure and cannot be handled conservatively.

A counterexample succeeds only if the system:

- is explicit reasoning;
- cannot map to FAR primitives;
- cannot be derived through registered derived concepts;
- cannot be handled by conservative extension;
- requires a genuinely new primitive.

A claimed counterexample must identify the failed primitive mapping, failed derived-concept reduction, failed conservative-extension route, and the required new primitive.
