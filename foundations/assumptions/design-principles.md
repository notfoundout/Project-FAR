# Design Principles

## Purpose

This document establishes the design philosophy governing every component of Project FAR.

---

# Principle 1

Definitions precede conclusions.

---

# Principle 2

Assumptions remain explicit.

---

# Principle 3

Every claim shall possess a justification.

---

# Principle 4

Primitive concepts shall be minimized.

---

# Principle 5

Dependencies shall remain acyclic.

---

# Principle 6

Canonical theory shall remain separate from exploratory research.

---

# Principle 7

Every subsystem shall possess a clearly defined scope.

---

# Principle 8

Formal theories shall inherit from the shared meta-theoretic layer whenever possible.

---

# Principle 9

Extensions shall preserve compatibility with existing theory unless revision is explicitly justified.

---

# Principle 10

Architectural simplicity shall be preferred over unnecessary generality.
