# Q-001 — Do All Representations Require Interpretation?

## Status

Open Question

## Origin

- GP-008 — Representation Grounding Investigation
- GP-009 — Interpretation Grounding Investigation
- E-001 — Representation/Entity Separation Evidence

## Question

Is interpretation a necessary property of every representation, or can representations exist independently of any assigned interpretation?

## Motivation

The answer affects the grounding of representation, interpretation, meaning, and models.

## Related Knowledge Objects

- C-001 — Representation Is Distinct from Represented Entity
- E-001 — Representation/Entity Separation Evidence

## Candidate Answers

1. Every representation necessarily requires interpretation.
2. Representations may exist without interpretation.
3. Different classes of representations have different interpretation requirements.

## Resolution Criteria

This question should be resolved only after additional grounding investigations and external validation establish whether interpretation is intrinsic or relational.
