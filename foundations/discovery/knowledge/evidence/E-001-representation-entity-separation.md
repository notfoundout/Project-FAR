# E-001 — Representation/Entity Separation Evidence

## Status

Pilot Evidence Object

## Evidence Statement

Multiple grounding investigations independently separated representations from the entities, states, systems, or structures they represent.

## Produced By

- GP-005 — State Grounding Investigation
- GP-008 — Representation Grounding Investigation
- GP-009 — Interpretation Grounding Investigation
- GP-010 — Model Grounding Investigation

## Supports

- C-001 — Representation Is Distinct from Represented Entity

## Evidence Type

Cross-investigation convergence.

## Strength

Moderate.

## Supporting Observations

- GP-005: a state is not identical to its representation.
- GP-008: a representation is distinct from the represented entity.
- GP-009: interpretation is distinct from representation.
- GP-010: a model is distinct from the represented system.

## Limitations

The evidence is conceptual and repository-internal.

It has not yet been validated against external case studies.

## Open Questions

- Does every representation require interpretation?
- Can representation be reduced to structure plus intended denotation?
