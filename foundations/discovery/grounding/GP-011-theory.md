# GP-011 — Theory Grounding Investigation

## Status
Active Grounding Investigation

## Objective
Determine whether Theory is a primitive concept or a derived explanatory construct.

## Hidden Assumptions
- Every theory explains.
- Every theory predicts.
- Every theory is a model.
- Every framework is a theory.
- Every theory is true.

## Attack 1 — Theory vs Model
A model operationalizes assumptions.
A theory justifies or explains relationships.

Result:
Theory ≠ Model.

## Attack 2 — Theory vs Framework
A framework organizes concepts and methods.
A theory proposes explanatory relationships.

Result:
Theory ≠ Framework.

## Attack 3 — Theory vs Definition
Definitions specify concepts.
Theories relate concepts.

Result:
Theory ≠ Definition.

## Attack 4 — Theory vs Truth
A theory may be false, incomplete, provisional, or highly corroborated.
Truth status is not part of the definition of theory.

Result:
Theory ≠ Truth.

## Candidate Weak Characterization
A theory is provisionally characterized as:

> A structured system of explanatory claims relating concepts, entities, or phenomena under explicit assumptions and scope.

## Dependency Hypothesis
Definitions
↓
Relations
↓
Theory
↓
Models

## Interim Result
The strongest current separation is:

Theory ≠ Model ≠ Framework ≠ Definition ≠ Truth

Further validation against Project FAR's own theory documents is required before revision.
