# Phase II — Repository Grounding Program

## Phase Objective

Transition Project FAR from architectural design to evidence-driven grounding.

## Repository Freeze

The Phase I architecture is provisionally frozen.

No new foundational concepts, methodology rules, or architectural layers should be added unless repeated audits demonstrate a genuine deficiency.

## Approved Work Categories

Every task must belong to one of four categories:

1. Artifact Audit
2. Grounding Investigation
3. Compression Pass
4. Revision

Revision shall occur only after supporting audits, investigations, and compression analysis.

## Primary Success Metric

Progress is measured by:

- grounded concepts;
- audit coverage;
- successful reductions;
- methodology compression;
- explicit research debt reduction.

Repository growth is not itself a success metric.

Phase II concludes when every canonical artifact has undergone at least one audit, every Tier 1 concept has completed a grounding investigation, and methodology revisions are driven by demonstrated evidence rather than architectural intuition.
