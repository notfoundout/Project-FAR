# Accepted Results

## Purpose

This document records research results that have survived investigation and are sufficiently stable to guide further development.

Acceptance within this document does not imply canonical status.

Rather, these results are regarded as the current working foundation of FARO pending formal proof.

---

# Accepted Result 1

## Candidate Primitive Operations

Current evidence supports the following candidate primitive operations.

- Inspect
- Evaluate
- Transform

Construct is presently regarded as a candidate derived operation.

---

# Accepted Result 2

## Derived Operations

Current evidence suggests that numerous operations are derivable through compositions of the candidate primitive operations.

Examples include:

- Compare
- Verify
- Analyze
- Learn
- Normalize
- Construct

These derivations remain subject to formal proof.

---

# Accepted Result 3

## Candidate Operational Architecture

Current evidence favors a minimal operational architecture consisting of:

- Inspect
- Evaluate
- Transform

Operational minimality remains under investigation.

---

# Accepted Result 4

## Candidate Mathematical Interpretation

Current investigations favor representing FARO operations as mathematical functions.

This interpretation remains provisional pending formal justification.

---

# Accepted Result 5

## Candidate Operational Calculus

Current investigations favor viewing FARO as an operational calculus rather than a collection of independent procedures.

This characterization remains provisional.
